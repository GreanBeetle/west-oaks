Governing Documents


  • Declarations

    These documents were filed with the State when West Oaks Condos were first proposed. In addition to describing the project, both documents set out the rules under which WOC runs. This document is equivalent to the "Conditions, Covenents, and Restrictions," i.e. CC&Rs, that govern planned communities.

  • House Rules

    The WOC board has adopted a set of "house rules". These are interpretations and clarifications of the more formal governing documents, and also add some additional points intended to make living at West Oaks Condos even more enjoyable for all. (The provisions in the House Rules are just as enforceable as the more formal conditions.)

Insurance Policies for WOC


The HOA is required to maintain hazard insurance for the structures and common areas.


The HOA also requires Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance for the Board of Directors. Owners wishing to review this policy should contact the West Oaks board. A photocopy charge may be assessed for any hard copies.
