How are my dues calculated?


Owners pay dues (assessments) based on their declared interest (percent ownership) in the common elements (see declaration here). The total annual budget is divided amongst owners based on this declared interest. See the 2021 dues schedule here.


What is the reserve fund for?


The board is required by Oregon state law to establish and fund a reserve account. This banks money for the eventual replacement of association property that is likely to wear out or require refurbishment: things like exterior paint, roofs, parking lot paving, sidewalks, etc. The board reviews and updates the reserve study annually, and may occasionally have a new reserve study conducted. The most recent comprehensive reserve study was done by PONO Building Consultants in 2020. You can read the full study report here.
